August 19, 1963
TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN
I hereby state and do certify that Daniel H. Maxson is qualified
to teach Spanish. He has known the Spanish language since a child
having lived in South America for nineteen years and has taught
Spanish for twelve years on the secondary level in the United
States.
Mr. Maxson has twenty -one quarter hours of Spanish, twelve of
them taken on the graduate level.
Very sincerely yours,
J. W. Cassell, Jr.
Academic Dean
