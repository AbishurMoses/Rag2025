October 30, 1963
Mr. John Siemens
Orangewood Academy
13732 Clinton Avenue
Garden Grove, California
Dear Mr. Siemens:
I am pleased to inform you that the faculty of Southern Missionary
College has presented your name to the Woodrow Wilson National
Fellowship Foundation for consideration. The faculty felt that you
represent the type of student that is representative of high academic
and citizenship requirements for a Woodrow Wilson award.
We trust the foundation will see fit to select you as a recipient of a
Woodrow Wilson National Fellowship. In the near future you will
receive more detailed instructions from the foundation asking for
extensive information. about you and your professional goals.
We wish you the best of success as a candidate for this honor.
Sincerely yours,
J. W. Cassell, Jr.
Academic Dean
