February 4, 1963
Mr. Robert H. Sanborn
Baker Memorial Section
Massachusetts General Hospital
Boston, Massachusetts
Dear Brother Sanborn:
We are very pleased to learn of your improved condition. We
understand that the operation was a success and you are recovering
nicely. It seems that the human body has a tremendous recovery
factor in the hands of God.
We sincerely wish for you a speedy recovery in order that you may
return to your home able to look forward with hope to a more pleasing
life.
The weather has tuned warmer and with the sun shining full bast
today, it seems that spring is not far away. Hurry home and join
us again in our community life.
Cordially your friend,
Wilbert M. Schneider
Academic Dean
